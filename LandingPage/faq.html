Frequently Asked Questions - GNOME™

What colors are available?

GNOME™ offers our product in: Green, Yellow, Orange, and Blue.

Can I test the temperature of my plants as well?

Yes! Your Gnome will give you all the necessary information regarding your plant, including temperature.

How many plants per Gnome?

Since we strive in efficiently tracking your plants quality, we assign one gnome per plant.

Do I need to be near my plants to record it?

Not at all! With our app, you will be able to remotely see your plant's information. All that is required is you have our app installed and the Gnome turned on.

I'm having Troubles Setting it Up

No worries! Watch our Video to learn how to set-up each gnome quickly and easily!
