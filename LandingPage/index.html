The Gnome
That Knows

Gnomes lives with your garden and becomes best friends with your plants. Gnome finds out if your plants are happy or sad and lets you know if they are thirsty too. Gnome will even tell you when your plants want to tan in the sun or hang out in the air conditioning.

GNOME™ Features

  • Display all the data on our easy-to-use app!
  • Record and alert your plants moisture level to improve life span.
  • Find out the optimal and current temperature of the area surrrounding your plant.
  • Track the illumination levels on each plant.
  • Choose from a variety of colors to bring life to your garden!
